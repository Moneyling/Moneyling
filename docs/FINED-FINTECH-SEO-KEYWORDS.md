# FinEd, FinTech & Financial Education – Buzzwords & SEO Keywords (US)

Research summary for making Moneyling.org on-topic and findable for financial education, FinTech, and FI/community outreach searches. Use these in page titles, meta descriptions, headings, and body copy where they fit naturally.

---

## 1. Financial Education (FinEd) – Schools & Policy

**High-value terms (policy & curriculum):**
- **Financial literacy** (very high search volume; use consistently)
- **Personal finance education** / **personal financial education**
- **Standalone personal finance course** (proven model in state policy)
- **Financial literacy standards** / **National Standards for Personal Financial Education**
- **Jump$tart** / **Jump$tart National Standards** (you already use this; keep it)
- **Standards-aligned** / **standards-aligned financial literacy curriculum**
- **K-12 financial education** / **high school financial literacy** / **middle school financial literacy**
- **Equitable access** (to financial education)
- **Financial literacy legislation** / **state financial literacy requirements**

**Topic areas (from Jump$tart / CEE standards):**
- Earning income | Spending | Saving | Investing | Managing credit | Managing risk

**Audience & implementation:**
- **Financial literacy for students** / **financial education for students**
- **Teacher financial literacy resources** / **educator financial literacy**
- **School financial literacy program** / **financial literacy in schools**
- **Story-based financial literacy** / **financial literacy curriculum**
- **LMS** / **learning management system** (for financial education)
- **COPPA compliant** (you already use this; keep it)
- **Digital financial literacy** / **financial literacy in a digital age**

**Sources:** NGPF 2025 State of Financial Education, NCSL 2025 legislation, Jump$tart/CEE National Standards, GAO Financial Literacy in a Digital Age, ExcelinEd 2025 landscape analysis.

## 2. FinTech & General Financial Education (SEO)

**High-volume keywords (use in meta, H1/H2, key paragraphs):**
- **Financial literacy** (~33K+ monthly searches)
- **Financial education** (1K+ monthly searches)
- **Personal finance** (5K+)
- **Financial planning** (5K+)
- **Financial wellness** (increasingly used by employers and FIs)
- **Money management** / **financial skills**

**FinTech-adjacent (if relevant to your product):**
- **Mobile banking** | **Digital payments** | **Online banking**
- **Budgeting apps** / **financial education app**
- **Robo-advisor** (you’re not one; avoid positioning as such, but “not a robo-advisor” is fine)
- **Financial literacy app** / **financial education platform**

**Long-tail (good for pages and FAQs):**
- **Financial literacy program for schools**
- **Financial education for high school students**
- **Financial literacy curriculum for teachers**
- **Free financial literacy resources for educators**
- **Financial literacy for credit unions** / **financial education for banks**
- **CRA financial literacy** / **CRA-eligible financial literacy**

**Content angle:** Focus on **user intent** (e.g. “financial literacy for educators,” “financial education for community outreach”) and **E-E-A-T** (experience, expertise, authoritativeness, trust) in copy and structure.

**Sources:** Fintech keyword research (LMG, SEO Labs, Grind Success, SJ Digital Solutions).

## 5. Phrases to Use More (and a few to avoid)

**Use more:**
- Financial literacy | Financial education | Personal finance education
- Standards-aligned (curriculum) | Jump$tart | National Standards
- K-12 | Middle school / High school financial literacy
- Credit union / Bank financial education | CRA | Community outreach
- Member financial wellness | Financial education ROI | Engagement metrics
- Story-based financial literacy | Financial literacy curriculum | LMS
- Financial wellness | Money management | Financial capability

**Use carefully or avoid:**
- **FinTech** – use only where you mean tech/digital delivery of financial education, not lending/payments.
- **Robo-advisor, budgeting app** – only to clarify you’re not one (as you already do).
- **Cryptocurrency, blockchain** – only if you cover them; otherwise skip.

---

## 6. Quick wins

1. **Meta descriptions** – Ensure each main page uses 1–2 of the high-value terms above (financial literacy, financial education, personal finance, CRA, credit union, etc.).
2. **Titles** – Include “Financial Literacy” or “Financial Education” in at least the Home and For Educators titles.
3. **For Financial Institutions** – Add “CRA,” “community outreach,” “member financial wellness,” “financial literacy for credit unions” in meta and first screen.
4. **For Educators** – Add “standards-aligned,” “Jump$tart,” “K-12 financial education,” “financial literacy curriculum” in meta and key headings.
5. **One “Financial literacy” or “financial education” in the first paragraph** on each audience page for both users and search.

Using these terms consistently in titles, meta, headings, and body copy will help the site stay on-topic and more findable for FinEd, FinTech-adjacent, and FI/CRA-related searches in the US.
